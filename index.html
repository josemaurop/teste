Simple Guacamole Recipe

A traditional Mexican dip made with fresh avocados

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp chopped cilantro (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 jalapeño, finely chopped (optional)

Instructions:

1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the seeds, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.

2. Mash with a fork to your preferred texture — smooth or chunky.

3. Add lime juice and salt, mixing gently.

4. Stir in chopped onion, tomato, cilantro, and jalapeño.

5. Taste and adjust seasonings. Serve immediately.

Why Guacamole?

Guacamole is one of the most iconic dishes in Mexican cuisine. It’s naturally healthy, vegan-friendly, and incredibly versatile — perfect with tacos, on toast, or as a dip for veggies and chips.

Fun fact: The name "guacamole" comes from the Aztec word *ahuacamolli*, meaning "avocado sauce."
