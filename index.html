Simple Guacamole Recipe

A fresh and tasty homemade dip!

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 small lime (juiced)
  • 1 small tomato (diced)
  • 1/4 small red onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 small jalapeño (optional, finely chopped)

Instructions:

1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the seeds, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.

2. Use a fork to mash the avocados to your desired consistency — chunky or smooth.

3. Add the lime juice and salt. Mix well.

4. Stir in the diced tomato, onion, cilantro, and jalapeño if using.

5. Taste and adjust seasoning with more lime or salt if needed.

6. Serve immediately with tortilla chips, toast, or as a topping for tacos or grilled meats.
